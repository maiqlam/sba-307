CheddarCon is an all-inclusive annual retreat where you have the opportunity to meet Cheddar, 13x Dog of the Year winner, and hang out with him doing his favorite things for a whole week!

CheddarCon offers various activities for those of all ages, such as day hiking, digging at the beach, and rolling in the grass. See the Activity Lineup for the full list of activities included in this package!

Cheddar is also available for booking outside of CheddarCon week to small parties. For more details, see Private Booking.
